Intro

This website is a compilation of resources that I've found during my time scouring the internet. It's a product of my boredom combined with my frustration at the lack of a centralized database of resources available online.

Since my primary interests are in the fields of mathematics, algorithmic programming, and cybersecurity, that's what the vast majority of resources on this website will relate to.

Anyways I'll stop yapping. Good luck on your journey to becoming an academic weapon!
